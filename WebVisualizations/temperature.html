Max Temperature



As expected, cities located closer to the equator (0 latitude) have a higher temperature. The plot points arch downwards as latitudes move further from the equator and temperatures drop. Of the cities examined, the coldest temperatures were recorded in the Northern Hemisphere and temperatures stayed relatively high in the Southern Hemisphere. There is also a wider range of temperatures in Northern Hemisphere cities however, due to the cluster of points on the right-hand side of the graph, it is clear more cities within the Northern Hemisphere were examined.
